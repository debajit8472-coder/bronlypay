Refund Policy

We strive to ensure a smooth and reliable experience for all users. This Refund Policy explains how refunds are handled within the application.

1. Failed Recharge or Transaction

In case a recharge, bill payment, or transaction fails due to technical reasons or operator issues, the corresponding amount will be refunded to the user’s wallet.

2. Refund Timeline

Refunds are typically processed within 3 to 7 working days. The actual time may vary depending on the payment method, bank, or service provider.
